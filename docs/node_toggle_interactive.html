Node-toggle turns a fixed +2.9% always-on transport tax into +0.54% (idle) … +2.83% (full), paid in proportion to the hooks you enable. Caveat: host-side cost overlaps the GPU replay → single-stream TPOT impact is small; the wins are host-CPU / throughput and high-frequency reconfigure.
